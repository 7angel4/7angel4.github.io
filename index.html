My research is driven by the role of probability in computer science as a unifying framework for uncertainty, verification, and decision making. I work on formal and quantitative verification of probabilistic systems, particularly under epistemic uncertainty, with a focus on robustness and multi-agent interaction. Motivated by recent advances in AI, I am increasingly interested in reinforcement learning under uncertainty and in bridging formal verification with learning-based methods, with the aim of providing robust, formally guaranteed solutions for autonomous and multi-agent systems.
